About

The Fit India Mobile App, developed by the Sports Authority of India, is a free, comprehensive fitness tool to help Indians assess and improve their health through age-appropriate fitness protocols, activity tracking (steps, water, calories, sleep), customized diet plans, and BMI calculation, making fitness accessible and encouraging a healthier lifestyle. Launched as part of the Fit India Movement, it allows users to set goals, monitor progress, and participate in fitness events, aiming to bring fitness into daily life for all citizens.
