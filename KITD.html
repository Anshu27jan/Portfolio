About

The Khelo India Talent Identification is primarily done through the Khelo India Rising Talent Identification (KIRTI) program, a nationwide initiative by India's Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports to find and nurture young sports talent (ages 9-18) using modern tech and global best practices, aiming to create a strong sports culture, identify future medalists, and combat gadget/drug misuse by assessing potential at centralized Talent Assessment Centres (TACs) KIRTI uses data analytics, AI, and standardized protocols to ensure a transparent, merit-based selection, identifying talent across 20 sports through systematic physical and skill assessments to support them in academies and build India's global sports image.
